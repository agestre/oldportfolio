Reach the Honey

This is a side-scroller game my partner Alana and I created using Scratch! In the game, you help a hungry bear named Bubsy collect honey by navigating through the holes in the honeycombs. Click on the picture to play!

Obamicon

This project takes a photo (that's a jpeg file) that the user inputs and applies a filter to it. It accomplishes this by going through all the pixels in the photo and changing its color depending on its RGB value. It then saves the new photo with the filter as "output.jpg."
